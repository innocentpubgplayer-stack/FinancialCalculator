Frequently Asked Questions

Are your financial calculators really free?

Yes, all our calculators and tools are completely free to use with no hidden charges or registration requirements. We believe financial education should be accessible to everyone.

Do you store my financial data?

No, we take your privacy seriously. All calculations are done locally in your browser, and we don't store any of your personal or financial information on our servers.

Can I use your calculators on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Our website is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices including smartphones and tablets. You can access our calculators anytime, anywhere.

How often are your calculators updated?

We regularly update our calculators to reflect changes in tax laws, interest rates, and financial regulations. Our team monitors financial news and updates our tools accordingly.
